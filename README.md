# Horizon

A tiny **macOS menu-bar app** that enforces the **20-20-20 rule** to reduce eye strain:
every **20 minutes** it gently takes over your screen for **20 seconds** with a calming
nature video, reminding you to look ~**20 feet** away and relax your eyes.

- Lives quietly in the menu bar — no Dock icon, no window.
- The break covers every display, blocks input so you actually rest, and closes itself after
  20 seconds (or via a tiny × in the corner).
- Background work (downloads, music, builds) keeps running the whole time.
- Automatically **skips breaks during calendar meetings** (via the macOS Calendar, which can
  sync your Google Calendar) **and while an app is in full-screen**.

> ⚠️ **Early development.** See [PLAN.md](PLAN.md) for the full design and [TODO.md](TODO.md)
> for live progress.

## Building from source

Requires **macOS** and **Xcode** (free, from the Mac App Store). Open the project in Xcode
and press **Run** (⌘R). Detailed, beginner-friendly steps will be added as the build
progresses.
